Bible Study Tools

Studies

There are several year-long studies for you to choose. Each study has a topic for every month of the year. There are Bible verses for each week of the month.

Even though these studies were written in years past, do not hesitate to (re)visit them as often as you feel led to do so.

Tips

  • Pray and seek wisdom and understanding from the Holy Spirit.
  • Read the Bible verse for the week multiple times to gain meaning and context.
  • Journal your thoughts for reflection later.
